RULES AND GUIDE

1

The Board

8x8 board, dark squares only. Each player starts with 12 pieces. White at bottom, Black at top.

8x812 pieces each
2

Moving

Tap a piece — green squares show where you can go. Regular pieces move forward diagonally one square.

tap to selectforward only
3

Capturing — Mandatory

Jump over an adjacent enemy to an empty square — that piece is removed. If a capture exists you MUST take it.

must capture
4

Chain Captures

After capturing, if another jump is available you MUST keep jumping in the same turn.

keep jumping
5

King

Reach the far end to become King (K). Kings move any distance diagonally in any direction.

any directionlong capture
6

Draw

Either player can offer a draw using the 🤝 button. The opponent can accept or decline. In online mode a draw offer is sent to your opponent.

mutual agreement
7

30-Second Clock

In PvP and Online modes each player has 30 seconds per turn. Red flash = under 10 seconds.

30s per turnauto-loss
8

Winning

Capture all opponent pieces, leave them with no moves, let their clock run out, or opponent resigns.
